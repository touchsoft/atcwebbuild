Privacy Policy for Clear Skies

Last updated: January 9, 2025

Overview

Clear Skies ("the App", "we", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users of our App.

Information We Do Not Collect

Clear Skies does not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data. The App operates entirely offline with respect to user data:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No gameplay data is transmitted
  • No analytics or tracking is performed
  • No user accounts are created or required

Local Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device:

  • Game progress and high scores
  • Achievement unlocks
  • Settings and preferences
  • Level unlocks

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. We have no access to this information.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information as outlined in their privacy policy:

  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • IP address
  • General location data

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

Apple In-App Purchases

The App uses Apple's In-App Purchase system for removing ads. We do not process or have access to any payment information. All transactions are handled securely by Apple.

For more information, see Apple's Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Clear Skies is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal data, children can safely enjoy the App.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You can reset your local game data at any time by uninstalling and reinstalling the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
